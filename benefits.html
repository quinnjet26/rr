Qualifications for Membership

Must have at least 5 years of experience in the Recognition Industry. For the purpose of the clarification the definition of “Recognition Industry” is the trophy, awards, ad specialty, premium, promotional products and incentive business.

A good credit standards and a satisfactory credit rating.

A good code of ethics in accordance with the written & board approved “Recognition Roundtable Code of Ethics”.

Maintain a retail store front with appropriate signage.

Agree to pay dues on a timely basis and any other accessed fees for special education or projects.

Agree as a condition of membership to abide by the by-laws of the Recognition Roundtable.

Be located in an available geographic area.

The membership needs to pass the prospective member business by a 2/3 vote of the member businesses that cast a vote.

Minimum of $500,000 in sales.
